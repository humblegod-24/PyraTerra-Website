Eco-Education Blueprint

“Turning Heat Into Hope” — messages, policy, and MDG links

Logo & Slogan

Slogan: “Turning Heat Into Hope”

Logo (described): A glowing red mountain shaped like a flame with green roots at the base — representing the balance between destructive and life-giving forces.

Key Messages for Citizens
  • “Heat gives life — protect it, don’t waste it.”
  • “Nature’s fire can power the future.”
  • “Clean energy begins beneath our feet.”
NEEAP-inspired Policy

PyraTerra enforces an Eco-Energy Education Week, where citizens use a VR-based platform to simulate managing geothermal resources responsibly. Participants learn energy conservation, pollution control, and sustainable development through interactive challenges.

Relevant MDGs
  • MDG 7: Ensure environmental sustainability.
  • MDG 9: Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.
