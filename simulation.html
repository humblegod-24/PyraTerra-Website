Food Chains, Energy Conversion & Homeostasis

🌿 Food Chain (Linear Flow)

In PyraTerra, geothermal energy fuels the biosphere, sustaining thermosynthetic producers and the heat-adapted organisms that depend on them.

Level Organisms Description
Producer 🌿 Ember Moss, 🌺 Cinder Bloom Convert geothermal heat and minerals into biological energy.
Primary Consumers 🪲 Ember Beetles, 💧 Vapor Birds Feed on producers; transform heat energy into movement and life.
Secondary Consumers 🦎 Lava Lizards Predators that feed on beetles and vapor birds, storing heat energy.
Tertiary Consumers 🦅 Fire Eagles, 🐍 Magma Serpents Top predators maintaining ecosystem stability.
Decomposers 🍄 Lumin Spores, 🧫 Thermobacteria Vulcanis Recycle nutrients and ash, ensuring energy renewal for producers.
